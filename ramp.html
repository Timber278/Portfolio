Bed RampFOR THE DOG

Personal Project, Autumn 2020

We have a small dog that loves waking us up in the morning by jumping on to the bed and sometimes on cold night he'll want to get under the blanket However, due to small injury he got after jumping off the bed, he became afraid of jumping on to the bed and kept making noises during the night, trying to wake up us so we would give him a lift. This kept happening night after night, and drove me crazy. So, one day after being completely sleep deprived after a particularly cold night, I decided to build him a ramp.

I built the ramp from plywood, created small steps, added padding and piece of furniture fabric to make it easy on his paws. The ramp has hinges so that I will fold away if I want to put it under the bed and is handy when moving or reorganizing the furniture. It took a lot of time and treats to teach our 9-year-old dog to use the ramp, but now he's completely happy with it and we get to sleep without him waking us up during the night.
